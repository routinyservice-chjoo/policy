Privacy Policy

Routiny - Habit Tracker
📌 Summary
Routiny is an ad-free, clean app.
We do not collect any personal information. All habit data is stored only on your device.

1. Data Collection and Use

1.1 Information Stored Locally

Routiny stores the following information on your device to provide app functionality:

Item Purpose Storage Location
Habit names, settings Habit management features User's device (Local DB)
Habit completion records Statistics and calendar display User's device (Local DB)

1.2 We Do Not Collect Personal Information

Routiny does not operate any servers and will never collect or transmit the following information:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, email, or phone number
  • Location data (GPS)
  • Device identifiers (Android ID, ADID, etc.)
  • Contacts or media files

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

🚫 No Ads
This app does not use any advertising services, including Google AdMob.
Therefore, we do not collect or track any advertising identifiers.

We do not use any third-party SDKs (Software Development Kits) that collect user data.

3. Sharing Feature

Routiny provides a feature that allows users to share their habit achievements on social media.

📤 About the Sharing Feature
• Sharing is initiated only by the user's direct action.
• Shared content includes: habit name, achievement status, app branding.
• The app does not separately collect or store shared content.
• Sharing uses the device's native sharing function and involves no third-party SDK data collection.

4. Data Storage and Deletion

Storage: All data is securely stored on the user's device (using Hive Database).

Deletion: No account cancellation process is needed. Uninstalling the app will immediately and permanently delete all data stored on the device.

5. App Permissions

Routiny requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its features.

Permission Purpose Required
Notifications Send habit reminders at user-set times (local notifications) Optional

* The app's core features (tracking) are available even without notification permission.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since the app does not require or collect any personal information, children can use it safely.
